Welcome to Collaborative Hackathon Fall 2024

The Collaborative Hackathon Fall 2024 brings together students from Virginia State University, National Taipei University, and Technical University of Moldova to solve real-world challenges using AI, IoT, cloud computing, data analytics, and other innovative technologies. This event mirrors real-world scenarios, where international collaboration is key to solving complex problems.

The registration is already started. The hackathon will formally start on Nov 4, 2024 on the unveilling of the problem set and will conclude on Nov 11, 2024 with the presentation of the solutions created by the participating teams!

Objectives of the Hackathon

  • Encourage cross-cultural collaboration between students from three countries.
  • Challenge students with technical and design problems requiring innovative solutions.
  • Provide real-world experience by simulating international teamwork in software engineering environments.
  • Enhance communication skills across time zones and cultural contexts.

Why Participate?

Gain real-world experience, network with peers and professionals, enhance your resume, and win exciting prizes. Showcase your creativity and collaboration skills across time zones!

Challenges

On November 4, 2024, three exciting design challenges will be revealed. Each team will have one day to choose a challenge they are most passionate about solving and inform the organizing team. Once confirmed, teams will spend the week collaborating, brainstorming, and developing solutions to their selected challenge.

Prizes

Exciting electronics and cash prizes will be awarded to the winning teams.

Judging Criteria

Submissions will be evaluated based on the following:

  • Information Gathering and Gap Analysis (15%)
  • Proposed Solution (20%)
  • Design and Usability (15%)
  • Feasibility (15%)
  • Creativity (10%)
  • Collaboration and Presentation (25%)

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can participate?
Students from Virginia State University, National Taipei University, and Technical University of Moldova.
What kind of skills do I need?
Basic knowledge in computer science, IoT, AI, and data analytics is useful but not mandatory. Teamwork and creativity matter the most!
How do I form a team?
Teams are composed of two students from each university. After registering, you will have access to a list of participants. Reach out via the Slack channel or email to form or join a team.
