Welcome to Collaborative Hackathon Fall 2024

The Collaborative Hackathon Fall 2024 brings together students from Virginia State University, National Taipei University, and Technical University of Moldova to solve real-world challenges using AI, IoT, cloud computing, and data analytics.

Finalize the dates for the hackathon!

Why Participate?

Gain real-world experience, network with peers and professionals, enhance your resume, and win exciting prizes. Showcase your creativity and collaboration across time zones!

Challenges

Three design problems will be unveiled on Nov 4, 2024:

  • Challenge 1: Technical Innovation – Leverage AI and IoT to solve a healthcare problem (50 points).
  • Challenge 2: Sustainable Design – Create solutions for smart cities using data analytics (50 points).
  • Challenge 3: User Experience – Design user-friendly apps for memory-related conditions (100 points).

Prizes

Exciting cash prizes and electronics will be awarded to the winning teams.

Judging Criteria

Submissions will be evaluated based on the following:

  • Information Gathering and Gap Analysis (15%)
  • Proposed Solution (20%)
  • Design and Usability (15%)
  • Feasibility (15%)
  • Creativity (10%)
  • Collaboration and Presentation (25%)

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can participate?
Students from Virginia State University, National Taipei University, and Technical University of Moldova.
What kind of skills do I need?
Basic knowledge in AI, IoT, or data analytics is useful but not mandatory. Teamwork and creativity matter most!
How do I form a team?
Teams are automatically formed with two students from each university after registration.
